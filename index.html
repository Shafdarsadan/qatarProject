Who We Are

About Luan Trading


Luan Trading Contracting & Services is a specialized HVAC company delivering expert air conditioning installation and maintenance services across Qatar. Backed by a team of highly skilled engineers and technicians, we are committed to providing reliable, efficient, and high-quality solutions tailored to every client's needs.

As an authorised dealer for globally renowned brands including Mitsubishi Electric, Daikin, Samsung, Midea, GREE, Blue Star, Frego, Carrier, and Toshiba — we supply and install systems that meet the highest international standards.

From private villas and schools to large commercial complexes, our end-to-end service covers design, supply, installation, commissioning, and long-term maintenance across Qatar.
